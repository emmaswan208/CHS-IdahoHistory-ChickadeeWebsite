Black-capped Chickadees can be found in many mixed forests across the United States and up into Canada. As well as mixed forests, the chickadees can be found in deciduous forests and open woods. Black-capped Chickadees will live in parks, cottonwood groves, bushes, etc. They can be found in residential areas as well. These birds choose dense areas to protect themselves from the wind and predators. Black-capped chickadees are attracted to nest boxes and feeders which will draw them to suburban yards.
