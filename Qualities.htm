Feathers

Black-capped Chickadees have a black stripe on the top of their head and on their neck. They have a white stripe on the side of their face which goes onto their chest and fades into a tan. They have grey backs which carries onto their wings. Their wings are a mix of white, grey, and black. Black-capped chickadees have black eyes and small beaks.

Mating

Unlike most birds, Black-capped Chickadees will keep the same mates for the rest of their life. This makes sense due to their short lifespan.

Fun Fact

Like squirrels and chipmunks, Black-capped Chickadees will hide nuts, each in a different place. They can remember up to 100 different hiding places.
